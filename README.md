## <img src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/212284087-bbe7e430-757e-4901-90bf-4cd2ce3e1852.gif" width="25" /> &nbsp;About Me

<img align="right" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/74038190/229223263-cf2e4b07-2615-4f87-9c38-e37600f8381a.gif" width="280" />

I'm a **Mobile Developer** based in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam 🇻🇳

I specialize in building **cross-platform mobile applications** using Flutter & Dart. I've worked with **Korean clients** in outsourcing environments, collaborating remotely with international teams — designers, backend developers, and project managers — to ship production-ready apps.

**What I bring to the table:**

-  📱 Cross-platform mobile apps with **Flutter & Dart**
- 🏗️ **Clean Architecture** — proper separation of Models, Services, Controllers & UI
- 🎯 State management: **GetX · Provider · Bloc · Riverpod**
- 🎨 **Figma-to-Flutter** — translating designs into pixel-perfect, responsive UIs
- 🌏 Remote collaboration with **Korean clients** via Slack
- ⚡ Performance optimization & complex debugging
- 📐 Experience with **Kotlin** (native Android) & **React Native**
